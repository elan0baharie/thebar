How this game works:

  1. First, Review the drink menu on the right. You don't wanna look like a fool behind the bar once doors open!
  2. Once you Enter your name and select an avatar you are ready to go!
  3. Next, you'll take customer orders by clicking on Next Customer.
  4. Select the ingredients and serve the customer with a smile!
  5. Keep an eye on your bank. Make enough money... who knows... maybe you'll move on to bigger and better things. Fair warning, waste the bar's liqour and that drink is coming out of your pocket!
